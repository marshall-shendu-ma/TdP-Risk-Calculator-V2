TdP Risk Calculator

This tool estimates Torsades de Pointes (TdP) risk based on in vitro field potential data.

Parameter Definitions

  • Cmax (nM): Maximum plasma concentration
  • Arrhythmia Type: no arrhythmia, type A, other
  • Cell Type: numeric indicator of hiPSC-CM source
  • Assay Time: 30 min or 24 hours
  • FPDc Pairs: Concentration-FPDc pairs for Hill fitting
